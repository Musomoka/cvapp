## 🎯 Quick Selection Guide

### Choose Based on Industry:

**Technology & Startups**
→ Modern (purple, contemporary)
→ Minimal (clean, focus on skills)

**Corporate & Finance**
→ Classic (traditional, conservative)
→ Professional (executive, sophisticated)

**Creative & Design**
→ Creative (bold, unique)
→ Modern (colorful, contemporary)

**Consulting & Management**
→ Professional (executive, two-column)
→ Classic (traditional, authoritative)

**Writing & Content**
→ Minimal (typography-focused)
→ Classic (traditional, readable)
