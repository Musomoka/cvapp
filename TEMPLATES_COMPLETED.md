## 📱 Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (>1024px)
- **Modern/Classic/Minimal:** Single column, full width
- **Creative:** Sidebar (280px) + Main content (flex)
- **Professional:** Two columns (2:1 ratio)

### Tablet (768px - 1024px)
- **Modern/Classic/Minimal:** Same as desktop, adjusted padding
- **Creative:** Sidebar stacks on top
- **Professional:** Sidebar stacks on top

### Mobile (<768px)
- **All templates:** Single column
- **All templates:** Reduced font sizes
- **All templates:** Dates stack below headers
- **Creative:** Full-width sidebar at top

## 🚀 How to Use

### For Users:
1. Go to **Step 1: Setup**
2. Click **View All Templates**
3. Choose any of the 5 templates:
   - Modern (purple, contemporary)
   - Classic (black/white, traditional)
   - Minimal (gray, clean)
   - Creative (red sidebar, bold)
   - Professional (teal, two-column)
4. See live preview update instantly!
5. Fill in your details
6. Download as PDF

### For Developers:
Templates are switched via the `template` prop in CVPreview:
```jsx
<CVPreview data={cvData} template="minimal" />
// Options: 'modern', 'classic', 'minimal', 'creative', 'professional'
```
